# Getting Started

Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA) is the part of computer science that studies **how to organise data** and **how to process it efficiently**. Choosing the right structure and the right algorithm is often the difference between a program that finishes instantly and one that never finishes at all.

Start here:

- [Introduction](intro.md) — what DSA is and why it matters.
- [A Simple Algorithm](simple-algorithm.md) — write your very first algorithm and meet the idea of time complexity.
